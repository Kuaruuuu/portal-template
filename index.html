About Me

Hello, my name is Hazel, I like to go by peaceofthestars online. A little bit about myself is that I am a passionate programmer and achieve my goals through love for the end goal and the process. Things are I enjoy working on are, rendering systems, shaders, systems and applications for various intentions (an example would be a user interface in a game engine that can work collaboratively with an independent standalone editor for artists to create and sample them visually in game or in program). I also love art and design, I am passionate to pour my intention of recreating nature visually and an artistic spin on wether its graphical code, or graphical art, I love games and rendering.

To go further into detail, I am a self taught graphics programmer that utilizes c++ and hlsl to create rendering technology that while uses known techniques aims to improve upon existing technology. I treat every new endeavour as a puzzle to solve, learning its fundamental foundational structure then utilizing that knowledge to build what I need. I enlighten creativity through artistic intention and efficient systems that perform well in various aspects wether its UX, cpu / gpu performance, and comprehendability of code and or systems for other developers. My latest project "Starudst" is a game but as of now is just rendering technology being built for the project. Its current goals, Approximate realtime lighting effects seen in real life in-renderer, and provide a user experience that is easy to work with for artists. Completed tasks are, physically based rendered light sources that utilize deferred / forward + rendering methodologies. Projected shadowmaps that utilize a penumbra PCSS shadow filter to begin the estimation of how shadows lose definition as distance grows away from the source of the cast.
